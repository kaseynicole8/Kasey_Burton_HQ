# How To Use This Repo

This is your artist headquarters. You do not need to know how to code to use it.

Think of each folder as a room:

- Brand room: who Kasey is and what she stands for.
- Music room: where songs, lyrics, and release notes live.
- Social room: where post ideas and caption ideas go.
- Business room: where money, gigs, and opportunities are tracked.
- Weekly room: where each week gets focused.
- Assistant room: where Codex learns how to help you.

## Your Weekly Flow

1. Open `08-weekly-planning/this-week.md`.
2. Tell Codex: "Walk me through my weekly reset."
3. Pick one main music goal, one money goal, one content goal, and one personal care goal.
4. Keep the week simple enough that you can actually do it.

## Easy Codex Requests

Use plain language:

- "Help me plan this week."
- "Add this song idea to my catalog."
- "Turn these lyrics into a song story."
- "Make me a content plan for this song."
- "Help me decide what to post today."
- "Help me make social media feel more rewarding."
- "Help me update my budget."
- "Help me plan a single release."

## Minimum Version Rule

If a plan feels too big, ask Codex:

```text
What is the minimum version I can do today?
```

Consistency matters more than perfection.
