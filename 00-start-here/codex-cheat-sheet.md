# Codex Cheat Sheet

You can talk to Codex like a person.

## Planning

- "Plan my week using my Artist HQ."
- "Help me focus on the most important next step."
- "Make this less overwhelming."
- "Give me the small version and the ambitious version."

## Music

- "Catalog this song."
- "Help me decide if this should be a single."
- "Write a song story for this."
- "Build a release plan around this song."

## Content

- "Give me 10 post ideas for this song."
- "Make these captions sound like me."
- "Create a filming day checklist."
- "Turn this messy thought into a TikTok idea."

## Business

- "Track this expense."
- "Help me find paid opportunities."
- "Write a booking email."
- "Make a budget I will actually follow."
