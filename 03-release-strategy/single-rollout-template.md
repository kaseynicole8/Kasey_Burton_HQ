## Four-Week Rollout

### Week 1: Tease

- Share the song concept.
- Post one lyric.
- Tell the story behind why it matters.

### Week 2: Invite

- Announce title or cover.
- Ask audience a question related to the song.
- Start email list or pre-save push.

### Week 3: Build

- Share chorus clip.
- Post behind-the-scenes video.
- Explain one key lyric.

### Week 4: Release

- Release day post.
- Story series.
- Live/acoustic version.
- Thank-you note.

## After Release

- Song story post.
- Fan reaction post.
- Acoustic/live version.
- Pitch to playlists, blogs, and local media.
