# Project Arc

## Phase 1: Sweet Girl Next Door

The first two projects should build trust, warmth, and the bright Kasey world.

Focus:

- Bubbly, likable, girl-next-door energy.
- Catchy pop/country songs.
- Positive but still emotionally honest.
- Make people feel welcome in The Sunshine Club.

## Phase 2: Sunshine But Honest

The next project can become more mature, real, and open.

Focus:

- Messy Christian girl honesty.
- Real struggles without glorifying sin.
- Bigger emotional storytelling.
- More confidence in the contrast between bubbly and deep.

## Phase 3: Full Artist Identity

Joy and heartbreak can coexist. Faith and questions can coexist. Sass and softness can coexist.

Focus:

- Stronger point of view.
- Deeper songwriting.
- Clear community around girls who need hope, truth, and a sister.
