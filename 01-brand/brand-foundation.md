# Brand Foundation

## Working Brand World

Kasey & The Sunshine Club.

## Core Positioning

Kasey Burton makes girl-talk pop/country for the girl trying to find herself, keep her faith, heal, laugh, grow, and remember she is not alone.

## Short Version

Girl-talk country/pop for messy girls with priceless souls.

## Brand Essence

Sunshine, but honest.

Not fake-happy. Not perfect-Christian-girl. Bubbly, sisterly, faith-rooted, emotionally real, sassy, kind, and willing to talk about life as it actually feels.

## Audience

The girl trying to find herself and navigate life. The girly girl. The real and not always perfect Christian girl. The person who needs a sister or bestie to say, "You're not alone in that."

## Brand Pillars

### Sunshine

Bubbly, joyful, playful, girly, hopeful.

### Sisterhood

Best-friend energy, girl talk, people matter, no one gets left alone in their thoughts.

### Faith With Honesty

Christian values without pretending life is picture-perfect.

### Sass And Spark

Upbeat country/pop confidence with humor, wit, and a little fire.

### Healing And Self-Worth

Mental health, self-care, empathy, boundaries, and the belief that souls are priceless.

## Brand Promise

Kasey's music helps people feel seen, loved, and less alone without dragging them into darkness.
