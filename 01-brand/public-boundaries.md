# Public Boundaries

These are open decisions. You do not have to answer all of them at once.

## Song Language

- Would Kasey curse in songs?
- If yes, which words are okay and which are not?
- Are there words that feel off-brand even if other artists use them?

## Modesty And Image

- What clothes feel confident and authentic?
- What feels too revealing or compromising?
- What is the difference between feminine confidence and sex appeal that does not fit the brand?

## Sex, Drinking, And Real Life

- Will Kasey sing about sex?
- Will Kasey sing about drinking?
- How can these topics be handled honestly without glorifying sin?
- What topics need care, nuance, or a redemptive angle?

## Faith And Honesty

- How openly does Kasey talk about being Christian?
- How does she say "messy Christian" in a way that feels humble, not performative?
- What does she want listeners to feel when hard topics come up?

## Current Anchor Statement

I am an imperfect person serving a perfect God, and I am also a sassy, silly, spontaneous, bubbly girl who loves life and people deeply.
