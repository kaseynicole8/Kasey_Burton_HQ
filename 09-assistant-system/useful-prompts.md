# Useful Prompts

## Weekly Reset

Walk me through my weekly reset. Help me choose one music goal, one content goal, one money goal, and one self-care goal.

## Song Catalog

Help me add this song to my song catalog and decide the next action.

## Release Planning

Help me decide whether this song should be a single and build a simple rollout plan.

## Content Planning

Give me content ideas for this song that sound like Kasey & The Sunshine Club.

## Budget

Help me look at this month's money and make a realistic budget.

## Opportunities

Help me find the best paid opportunities to pursue this month.
