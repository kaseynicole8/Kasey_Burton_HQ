# Kasey Context

Kasey Burton is an artist and songwriter in Nashville, Tennessee.

She makes pop/country music with bubbly, girly, sassy, heartfelt, faith-rooted, and emotionally honest energy.

## Brand World

Kasey & The Sunshine Club.

The brand essence is: sunshine, but honest.

Kasey wants to be a bright, sisterly artist for girls who are trying to find themselves, keep their faith, navigate mental health, heal, laugh, and remember they are not alone.

## Audience

The girl trying to find herself. The girly girl. The real and not always perfect Christian girl. Anyone who needs a sister or bestie to say their thoughts and feelings are not weird or lonely.

## Values

Kasey stands for kindness, Christian values, small-town values, valuing people, mental health awareness, self-care, empathy, and real-life conversations.

Kasey stands against cruelty, controlling behavior, rape culture, evil, meanness, and glorifying sin in music.

## Tone

Warm, playful, honest, encouraging, sisterly, clear, sometimes sassy, never fake-perfect.

## Career Needs

Kasey needs systems for focus, finances, release planning, content consistency, song cataloging, gig opportunities, and turning music into a sustainable career.

Because ADHD and self-employment can make structure difficult, plans should include simple next steps, minimum versions, and low-shame reset points.

## Assistant Rule

Help Kasey build momentum without overwhelming her. Keep things practical, kind, honest, and action-oriented.
