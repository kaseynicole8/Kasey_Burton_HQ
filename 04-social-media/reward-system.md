# Social Media Reward System

Social media needs to feel connected to a reward, not just pressure.

## Points

- 1 point: write a caption
- 1 point: film a draft
- 2 points: post a video
- 2 points: post a performance clip
- 3 points: send an email/newsletter
- 3 points: complete a filming day

## Rewards

- 5 points: coffee shop writing date
- 10 points: small creative purchase
- 20 points: outfit, prop, or self-care treat
- 35 points: photo/video shoot fund
- 50 points: studio or release investment

## Rule

The point is not to become a content machine. The point is to invite people into the music.
