# Filming Day Plan

## Before Filming

- Pick 1 song or theme.
- Pick 3 outfits or looks.
- Pick 5 simple ideas.
- Charge phone.
- Clean filming area.
- Set up light.

## Film These

- 2 acoustic clips
- 2 talking videos
- 2 lyric videos
- 1 funny/sassy video
- 1 honest girl-talk video
- 1 behind-the-scenes clip

## After Filming

- Save clips in one folder.
- Write quick notes about each clip.
- Pick the easiest one to post first.
